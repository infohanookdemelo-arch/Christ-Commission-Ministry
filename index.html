About Us

Christ Church Ministry is committed to fulfilling the Great Commission through worship, discipleship, and outreach.

Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 1995, Christ Church Ministry has been a beacon of hope and faith in our community. Our mission is to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ through authentic worship, biblical teaching, and compassionate service.

We envision a community transformed by the love of God, where every person experiences spiritual growth and finds purpose in serving others.

Our Mission

To make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

Our Vision

A world where every person knows the love of Christ and lives out their God-given purpose.
